Common Exercise

The following are common exercise that we encourage our clients to do as part of thier daily exercise routine.

Burpbee
Burpees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strengh and endurance. Being in a standing position, drop into a squat and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly, and then jump up from a squatted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strenght. To perform a plank, get in a push up position and res your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain climber are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up position , then bring one knee uo to your chest and then switch as quickly as you can(as though you are climbing a mountain).
